About Us
Bridging Global Business
with Odisha's Industrial Strength

Aurum Enterprises is a consulting liaison firm with offices in Mumbai and Bhubaneswar, specialising in facilitating trade and business partnerships between international and domestic companies and Odisha's vast industrial ecosystem.

We serve as your trusted on-ground partner — whether you are sourcing commodities, establishing supply chains, registering as a vendor with Public Sector Undertakings, or building long-term commercial relationships with Odisha's producers across sectors.

Our deep understanding of Odisha's industrial landscape, government processes, and commercial networks means you get direct access to decision-makers, without navigating the complexity alone.

Prof. Sarbeswar Mohanty

What We Do
End-to-End Liaison & Trade Facilitation
From your first enquiry to cargo on the vessel, we manage every step of your engagement with Odisha's industries.
⚖

Market Entry & Vendor Registration

We guide you through the registration and empanelment process with Odisha-based producers — PSU or private — handling documentation, compliance, and coordination to get you approved.

☰

Tender Monitoring & Bid Support

We track tenders across MSTC, CPPP, GeM, and private procurement portals. We analyse terms, flag risks, and support you through spot and long-term contract bidding.

★

Commercial Intelligence

Insights into pricing mechanisms, prevailing market terms, contract structures, and competitive landscape across Odisha's key industries.

☍

On-Ground Liaison

We represent you in meetings with producers, government bodies, and industry stakeholders across Odisha. From introductions to contract negotiations.

⚓

Logistics & Supply Chain

We coordinate shipping, inland transport, and port logistics through Paradip, Dhamra, Gopalpur, and Visakhapatnam.

⚙

Contract Review & Advisory

We analyse sale contracts, flag important commercial terms — force majeure, demurrage, laytime, payment terms — and advise on negotiation strategy.
